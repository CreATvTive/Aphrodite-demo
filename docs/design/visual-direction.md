# Aphrodite visual direction

Status: REFERENCE
Authority-Scope: Non-binding visual continuity
Supersedes: Visual production guide as a current authority
Superseded-By: —
Last-Verified: 2026-07-14

This reference preserves visual qualities that do not depend on the pre-chatbox state model: quiet independent presence, warm-gray and pale-amber light, low saturation, soft material response, restrained framing, and perception without service performance.

The visual surface should support the impression that Aphrodite already exists before interaction, notices without immediately serving, and remains bounded rather than becoming a companion-product or game-character display. Avoid commercial idol staging, hard promotional lighting, exaggerated feedback, decorative intimacy, and renderer effects that substitute spectacle for presence.

This document is not part of P1–P4 acceptance, does not require Live2D, and does not define motion parameters, state dimensions, field dynamics, language conditions, clamp/decay, baseline, or public/internal boundaries. It must not override [`../chatbox/phase-plan-v0.md`](../chatbox/phase-plan-v0.md).

The full historical production guide and Live2D prototype are preserved under [`../archive/visual-prototypes/`](../archive/visual-prototypes/).
